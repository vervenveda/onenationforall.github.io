Why I’m Running
I am running to defend, support, and represent the American People especially those who feel unseen by parties, donors, lobbyists, and permanent insiders. We refuse to be ruled by corruption any longer.

My first loyalty is to everyday families, workers, caregivers, veterans, and children. Transparency and staright talk, NO back-room deals. This campaign begins with a simple promise: I will answer to the citizens, not to the powerful, not the corporations, not to big PHARMA, not to the foreign countries draining our resources financially.
Pillar One
People Before Power

Every decision starts with one test: Does this protect and strengthen ordinary people? Working families, caregivers, small businesses, veterans, and children; not lobbyists and insider circles? The goal is to retain our sovereign rights: the freedom so many have fought and died for, the workforce wondering how they can survive another crushing year of price gouging, rising utility costs and polution, all while the politicians are living a very comfortable life on our dime.

Pillar Two
Clean Hands Governance

Full financial transparency, strict conflict-of-interest rules, and decisions explained in plain English. If it cannot be justified openly to the American people, it doesn’t belong in this campaign or in the Political Arena.

Pillar Three
Guarding the Future

We must protect the next generation from the debts, disasters, and divisions we are on track to hand them. That means dealing honestly with climate, technology, education, health, and the survival of real democracy, not just the next news cycle.
