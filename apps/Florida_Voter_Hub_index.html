Quick FAQ

Where do I check my registration and status?
Use the official Florida voter information lookup page (button above).
Where do I find my polling place?
Use the official precinct lookup (county links) to find your Election Day location.
Where do I get vote-by-mail and early voting info?
Vote-by-mail and early voting locations/timelines are posted on official state + county pages. Use the links above.
Where do I find official deadlines?
Use the state election dates calendar and your county Supervisor of Elections site for local specifics.
