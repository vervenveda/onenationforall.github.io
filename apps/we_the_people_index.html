We the People Learning Lab

A single-file, no-framework prototype for learning the U.S. Constitution, Bill of Rights, all 27 amendments, landmark legal concepts, and federal laws that affect daily life.

Education only. This portal teaches legal literacy and civic research skills. It is not legal advice and does not replace qualified legal counsel.

How the Lab works

The portal randomly draws civic scenarios, legal topics, amendments, and daily-life law areas. Learners compare facts, identify constitutional hooks, and use free primary sources to research the answer.
