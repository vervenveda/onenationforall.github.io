Red and blue make purple

The No Party Citizen movement

A civic identity for people who place conscience, constitutional responsibility, evidence, and the public good above automatic party loyalty.

Not politically homeless—politically sovereign.

No Party Citizen does not require everyone to think alike. It asks citizens to remain free enough to question every party, every candidate, every institution, and every claim.

Purple is not a demand for false compromise. It is the meeting place where honest differences can become workable solutions without dehumanizing neighbors.

People over partiesCitizens are not property of political organizations.
Evidence over slogansClaims should survive examination and source checking.
Service over statusPublic office is a duty, not a throne.
Unity without conformityCooperation does not require surrendering conscience.
